Ahi Tuna

Seared Ahi Tuna Steaks

Ahi Tuna is one of the easiest dinner you can make and it takes less than a minute to make

Ingredients

For the Tuna

  • 80oz Ahi Tuna Steak
  • 2 tablespoons white sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoons black sesame seeds
  • Salt

For the Sauce

  • 1.5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 tabelspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 tablespoon chili garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon oyster sauce
  • 1 lime, zested and juiced
  • 1/2 inch of ginger, grated

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together all the sauce ingredients until fully combined then set aside.
  2. Lightly season your tuna with salt. Then place the tuna steak in a small bowl (or ziplock bag) and pour half of your sauce on top. Let the tuna marinate in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, mix the black and white sesame seeds together and set aside.
  4. Remove the tuna from the marinade and lightly pat it dry. Then gently press the sesame seeds into the tuna (on all sides) until its fully coated. Once coated set aside.
  5. Heat a medium skillet over medium to medium-high heat and drizzle with high-heat oil. You want the pan to get hot but not too hot, otherwise the sesame seeds will burn before the tuna cooks. Once hot, cook the tuna for about 25 seconds per side.
  6. Once cooked, remove from the tuna from the pan, slice, and top with the remaining sauce.
